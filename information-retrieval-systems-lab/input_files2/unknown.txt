Simple Lemon Pasta Recipe

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package directions until al dente.

Meanwhile, in a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about one minute. Stir in the lemon zest and juice.

Drain the pasta, reserving one cup of the pasta water. Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the lemon sauce. Toss to combine, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a smooth sauce. Season with salt and pepper, and top with grated cheese.
