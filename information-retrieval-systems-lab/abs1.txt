In India, many government schemes offer vital financial support, resources, and welfare benefits. However, widespread unawareness, challenges with application processes, language barriers, and difficulty finding trusted information often prevent citizens from accessing these opportunities. This project addresses these issues by developing a user-friendly, mobile-based solution to centralize and clearly present scheme information. The primary goal is to make government schemes accessible to all citizens, irrespective of their literacy level or preferred language. Developed using cross-platform technology for both Android and iOS, the application currently utilizes a static JSON database. This database stores comprehensive scheme details—including titles, categories, descriptions, eligibility criteria, required documents, deadlines, and official links—in multiple languages such as English, Telugu, & many more. Key features include Text-to-Speech (TTS) for audio readouts of scheme details, aiding users with reading difficulties or visual impairments, and a notification system for upcoming deadlines. The simple and clear design ensures ease of use for diverse age groups and backgrounds. Initial testing confirms benefits like quick offline information access, valuable in areas with limited internet, while multilingual content and TTS enhance inclusivity. Future enhancements will focus on transforming the platform into a more complete, reliable, and intelligent resource. This includes transitioning to a dynamic cloud-based database, expanding scheme categories, and integrating WhatsApp/SMS alerts. Crucially, Artificial Intelligence (AI) will be leveraged to introduce features such as an intelligent scheme recommendation system tailored to individual user needs, natural language chatbot assistance for intuitive querying, and enhanced voice-enabled navigation. These AI-driven tools, alongside advanced search and filtering, aim to significantly improve accessibility, personalize the user experience, and empower citizens to easily stay informed and benefit from available schemes.
