In India, many government schemes are introduced to provide financial support, resources, and welfare benefits to citizens. However, a large number of people remain unaware of these opportunities or are unsure about the application process. Common challenges include language barriers, lack of awareness, and difficulty in finding accurate and complete information from trusted sources. This work focuses on building a mobile-based solution that gathers and presents scheme information in one place, in a clear and user-friendly way. The main purpose is to make government schemes more accessible to all citizens, regardless of their education level or preferred language. The system is developed using cross-platform technology so it can work on both Android and iOS devices. In its current stage, it uses a static JSON database to store details such as scheme titles, categories, descriptions, eligibility criteria, required documents, deadlines, and official website links in English and Telugu. A notable feature is Text-to-Speech (TTS), which reads out the scheme details in the selected language. This is especially useful for people who have difficulty reading or those who are visually impaired. Another feature is the notifications system, which alerts users about upcoming deadlines. The design is kept simple and clear so that people from all backgrounds and age groups can use it without difficulty. Testing has shown that the application provides quick offline access to information, making it valuable even in rural areas with poor internet connectivity. The bilingual content and TTS function increase inclusivity and usability. In the future, the system will be enhanced with special features and advanced functionalities such as a dynamic cloud-based database, more categories of schemes, WhatsApp/SMS alerts, multi-language support, advanced search and filtering options, and other innovative tools to improve accessibility and user experience etc. These improvements aim to make the platform a complete, reliable, and intelligent source for citizens to stay informed and benefit from available schemes.
